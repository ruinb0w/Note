## Glossary

### Measure0-3

M0 = cash

M1 = M0 + enterprise saving

M2 = M1 + all the saving

M3 = M2 + current assets(like bond, note)

### GDP(Gross Domestic Product)

### interest

simple interest: $c = p(1+tr)$

> c - count
>
> p - principle
>
> t - time
>
> r - rate

compound interest: $c = p(1+r)^t $

> c - count
>
> p - principle
>
> t - time
>
> r - rate
